01 — About

Who I am

I'm a quantitative biologist by training who discovered a deep love for R coding through scientific research. My journey has been predominantly self-taught — I developed my skills by working on real datasets and analytical challenges in academic research.

My work spans marine biology, microbiome research, and coral physiology. I've published research on coral symbiosis, built reproducible analysis pipelines, and contributed to the scientific community with open, transparent code.

Beyond research, I love sharing knowledge — I've created and run R workshops on data wrangling and reproducible research, and I'm always excited about new collaborations that make a difference.
